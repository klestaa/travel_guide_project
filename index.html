    Travel Wisdom

    Safety First

    Always check government travel advisories before booking. Register with your embassy and keep emergency contacts handy.

    Documents

    Ensure your passport is valid for 6+ months. Check visa requirements early and keep digital/physical copies of documents.

    Money Matters

    Notify your bank before traveling. Use ATMs for local currency and always have backup payment methods.

    Packing Smart

    Pack light with versatile clothing. Check weather forecasts and bring essentials like adapters and medications.

    Capture Moments

    Respect local customs when photographing. Ask permission and avoid sacred or sensitive areas for photography.

    Cultural Respect

    Learn basic phrases in the local language. Respect traditions, dress codes, and customs of your destination.
